About Us

ADIS LIFESCIENCES PVT LTD has come into inception in the Year 2011.

Who we are

It is a well managed Company with the Core Group comprising of Highly Skilled Professionals having Competency in Areas of Sales, Product Management, Training, Logistics & Human Resources. The Company is equipped with adequate Resource to help it Foray into the Industry with required backup of People & Material.


Why choose us?

Our Products are manufactured at the Plant with State of the Art facility which is accredited with ISO 9001:2000/cGMP certifications. Each Batch passes through stringent Quality Control steps to ensure high Standard of Quality & Safety. The initial marketing is aimed at Central & Southern part of the Country which would then gradually spread to North & West & subsequently in East.

Our Commitment

The Company offers a Conducive Work environment for professionals & an atmosphere that Nurtures the individual’s Competency & Potential which facilitates the opportunity of Vertical Growth in the Industry. ADIS LIFESCIENCES is committed to Partnering Healthcare with the Medical Fraternity & ensure Healthy Lives. In this Endeavour, ADIS LIFESCIENCES would support the fraternity with all possible resources including education to the Care Giver as well. Our Ideology would be to launch essential & cost-effective medicines for the Patient Population helping them to improve their Well-Being.
